📚 OpenStacks for Change

A living, modular repository of open-source toolchains and knowledge infrastructure for public interest work in India — built and maintained by Varna Sri Raman.

🔍 About This Project

OpenStacks brings together documentation tools, evaluation methods, visual ethnography, MEL frameworks, and research artifacts from real-world public health, gender equity, education, and climate resilience work. This is part of a broader open development effort to support Indian and South Asian social systems through actionable knowledge.
